Introduction

The Algosaibi Family, one of the most respected and well known in the Kingdom, has a long standing prominent position within the business community as one of the distinguished and successful Traders. Initially involved in traditional trading and pearling, the business eventually evolved into food supply and the Group, as of today, is one of the largest wholesale food distributors in the Middle East. Algosaibi Services is a Limited Liability Company with two principal share holders. Shares are held in the following proportions:

Khalifa Algosaibi Holding Company : 95%
Mr. Bader Khalifa Abdulrehman Algosaibi : 05%
