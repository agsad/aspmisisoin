Algosaibi Services (AGS), a subsidiary of the long-established and diverse Khalifa AlgosaibI Holding Co., was established in 1976 when the late Sheikh Khalifa Abdulrahman Algosaibi signed a joint venture between Khalifa Algosaibi Holding Co. of Saudi Arabia and Grand Metropolitan plc (GRANDMET) of the United Kindom. In 1985, Khalifa Algosaibi Holding Co. bought all the shares previously owned by its foreign partner, thereby making AGS, a 100% Saudi-Owned Company.

For several decades now, AGS has been a leading supplier of quality industrial and commercial catering services to large public and private sector clients. In addition to catering, AGS offers a list of support services including staff accommodation, janitorial services, duct-cleaning, preventative maintenance, gardening, and pest control.

AGS clearly understands that our Client is buying management, and we provide the highest quality available. Our expertise is best exhibited by the superior services with which we reliably present our clients on a regular basis. By tailoring our operating procedures to our Client’s needs, AGS is able to provide Clients with a structure consisting of a single contact point for all major administrative tasks, thereby relieving them of the pressure of juggling different vendors, each for a separate need. Our professional management team effectively controls cost, manpower performance, and schedules, in order to deliver maximum returns on all our Client’s investments.

AGS prides itself on its efficiency, flexibility, and reliability. By working with us, you are guaranteed consistent results conforming to the highest international standards of quality and safety.
