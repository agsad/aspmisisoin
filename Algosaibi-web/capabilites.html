Permanent Facilities

AGS have constructed permanent accommodation facilities at Dammam and Jubail. The Dammam facility is built for 1000 personnel, Jubail facility for 3000 personnel and Yanbu facility for 1500 personnel. Both facilities are designed to meet the needs and requirements of all range of residents, from Senior Management to blue collar work force. The facilities are in-built with large spacious dining area catering to various ethnic groups, central laundry, bakery, etc. in addition to other luxurious exigencies such as recreation, gymnasium, satellite connections, etc. In addition, AGS have acquired an Executive Western Compound at Jubail consisting of 25 furnished villas including swimming pool, tennis court, squash court, gymnasium, etc. Moreover, to meet the needs and requirements of our vast clientele, AGS have leased suitable facilities at Abqaiq, Riyadh, Jeddah and Yanbu.

Temporary Facilities

AGS have specialized in constructing temporary camps with portable buildings including complete infrastructure to international standards. The facilities are built to suit the requirements of clients giving the highest possible importance to safety standards.

AGS offer 100 % solution, from camp construction to demobilization which includes operation of the facilities, maintenance, utilities, catering, housekeeping, laundering, recreation, pest control and other related services, alleviating our clients from all the difficulties of coordinating the entire process with different entities.

So far, AGS have successfully accomplished TEN such operations to prestigious clients including Bechtel, Toyo Engineering and CB&I. .
